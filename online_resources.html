Online Resources

Site Use Case
Organic Syntheses One of the best sources of free, reliable chemistry papers about the synthesis of organic compounds. Each procedure is indepently reproduced before publication.
Directory of Open Access Journals A useful search engine for open-access journals with emphasis on the physical sciences, life sciences, and medicine.
MDPI Swiss published with more than 400 open-access journals in a variety of fields.
National Academies Press (NAP) Publisher for the US National Academies of Science, Engineering, and Medicine. Publications tended to be geared toward policymakers, but some cover cutting-edge technologies.
Open Textbook Library Hosts more than a thousand open textbooks from a variety of publishers, with content ranging from introductory to advanced.
IntechOpen Another open access published with a focus on books and book series in the sciences and humantities. A few open journals, too.
Survivor Library Repository for public domain books on almost every subject. Of particular note, it hosts a large selection of chemistry textbooks from the 19th and early 20th centuries.
Internet Archive Useful for tracking down archive copies of old blogs and websites that are no longer available anywhere else.
Zotero A free, open-source citation manager with robust API support.
W3 Schools Color Converter Converts RGB, Hex, CMYK, and other common and uncommon color formats. One of several free color tools by W3 Schools.
ARKIVOC An open-access journal of organic chemistry with a lengthy publication history
Reich Total Syntheses An index of total synthetic procedures for a wide variety of complex organic compounds, with references.
Bordwell pKa Tables Free pKa tables for substances in water, DMSO, and the gas phase, organized by functional group.
Named Reagents Names and molecular structures several hundred of the most common reagents used in organic chemistry.
Desmos A responsive online 2D graphing calculator
Gnuplot A free (not open source) plotting utility with a CLI and excellent documentation.
Falstad Hosts a variety of math and physics visualization tools, including a robust simulator for simple circuits.
PC Part Picker A great tool for getting started with building your first PC.
Embry-Riddle TRUST Exam A free exam site for the Recreational UAS Safety Test (TRUST), which is required for recreational drone flyers in the US.
Engineer's Edge Hosts a variety of physics and engineering reference sheets and calculators, physical properties of engineering materials.
Guerilla Guide to CNC and Resin Casting A guide to DIY CNC and resin casting.
EEVBlog A forum for electronics hobbyists.
Hackaday.io A site for hobbyist hardware hacking in all its forms. A few free courses, too.
Pexels High-quality, free stock photos for projects.
Vector Ruler Generator Generates a ruler in .svg form with your chosen dimenions. Useful for printing on transparencies, microscope work, etc.
Amazon Paint Finder Useful for visualizing paint colors with a foreground as you prepare for renovations.
Free Music Archive Free music from a variety of genres. Great for YouTube.
Incompetech A small selection of royalty-free music.
Inkscape A free vector graphics editor.
Coloring for Colorblindness A guide to using color on websites to support users with protanopia, deuteranopia, or tritanopia.
DaVinci Resolve Free, powerful video-editing software with a steep learning curve.
Photopea A free, browser-based image editor supporting common formats. Has ads.
Data Visualization Catalogue An index of data visualization techniques, with examples and instructions for implementing in various tools.
Periodic Table of Visualization Another index of data visualization techniques, in periodic table form.
Dataphys Physical visualization techniques, spanning ancient history to modern day.
Big-O Cheat Sheet Quick reference for time and space complexity of common algorithms.
Postman Widely-used tool with a free version for building and testing APIs.
Ngrok Free tool for personal projects, with a variety of uses. Especially useful for quickly spinning up a web server to test webhooks in a development environment.
File.org Index of file extensions and programs that support them.
ASCII-Code.com Quick reference for ASCII, DEC, OCT, HEX, BIN, and HTML number/name codes.
PythonKitchen List of free books on Python programming from a variety of publishers.
Design Patterns Design patterns in various programming languages, with code examples.
Matplotlib Free, powerful Python library for plotting, with support for multivariate plots, vector fields, gridded data, volumetric data, statistics, and more.
Processing Flexible sketchbook language geared toward visual arts.
OpenProcessing Repository for user-submitted Processing sketches.
Use the Index, Luke! A guide to SQL database performance for developers.
